About Max Muenke

Maximilian Muenke, M.D. is a US licenced Physician, a US licenced Physician Acupuncturist. He is interested in using hypnosis, acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine to take care of the whole person, not just the symptoms.

His goal is to promote optimal health and enhance the quality of life by retaining a balance between mind, body, and spirit, and in case of disease, by regaining this balance and a smooth flow of energy throughout the body. Read more »
